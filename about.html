About CNPSC

The Child Nutrition Program of Southern California (CNPSC) is a nonprofit organization and a sponsor of the USDA Child & Adult Care Food Program (CACFP). We partner with licensed family child care homes to help them serve nutritious meals that support children’s health and learning.

Founded in 1981 and based in La Mesa, California, our team provides training, resources, and technical assistance so providers can meet CACFP requirements with confidence.
