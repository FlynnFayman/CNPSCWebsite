Employment Opportunities

CNPSC periodically hires for roles supporting CACFP operations, provider training, and monitoring. If you’re passionate about child nutrition and quality early care, we’d love to hear from you.

How to express interest

  1. Prepare a brief resume highlighting experience in child care, nutrition, compliance, or community service.
  2. Call our office at (619) 465‑4500 for current openings and application steps.

CNPSC is an equal opportunity employer.
