Benefits of Joining CACFP with CNPSC

Reimbursement for Meals

Get reimbursed for eligible breakfasts, lunches/suppers, and snacks served to children in care—following CACFP meal patterns.

Clear Meal Patterns

Use simple meal pattern handouts for infants and children to plan creditable menus.

Training & Compliance

Annual training and guidance on recordkeeping, civil rights, and program rules keep you compliant.

Nutrition Education

Monthly “Nutrition Edition” newsletters with activities and recipes to share with families.

Claiming Tools

Support for electronic claiming platforms to make monthly claims easier.

Friendly Support

Phone help and resources when you need answers about menus, enrollment, or claims.
