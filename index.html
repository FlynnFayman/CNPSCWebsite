Our Motto is ...

"Healthy Meals For Healthy Children"

Child Nutrition Program of Southern California (CNPSC) is a sponsor of the Child Care Food Program (CCFP). CCFP is a USDA, federally funded program, and in California, is administered by the California Department of Education. CNPSC reimburses Child Care Providers for the nutritious meals served to the children in their care. The program means better nutrition for healthy bodies and growing minds.
